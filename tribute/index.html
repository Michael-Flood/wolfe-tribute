The Words and Worlds
of Gene Wolfe

1931 - 2019

The best science fiction
writer you've never heard of...

Gene Wolfe was one of the foremost speculative fiction writers of the 20th century. Little known beyond an intense and devoted fandom (which included many prominent authors such as Ursula K. LeGuin and Neil Gaiman), Wolfe's signatures are dense, allusive prose, unreliable narrators, and stories whose primary themes are memory and forgetting.

A prolific author, Wolfe published 31 novels in a career spanning 45 years.

For more about Wolfe's life and fiction, be sure to check out the Wolfe Wiki
